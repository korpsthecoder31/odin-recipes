Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • 125g Pecorino Romano
  • 125g Guanciale
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk (optional)
  • 7g fresh ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Fry Guanciale
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Mix in Egg
  5. Add Pepper
  6. Mix in mouth
  7. Eat
