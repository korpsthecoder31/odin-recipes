Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • 125g Pecorino Romano
  • 125g Guanciale
  • 1 can (750g) San Marzano whole tomatoes
  • 50ml white wine (optional)
  • 7g fresh ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Fry Guanciale
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Reduce Wine
  4. Add Tomatoes
  5. Add Cheese
  6. Add Pepper
  7. Mix in mouth
  8. Eat
